# **Data Analysis Training – Daily Diary (Day 16)**

**Date:** ___________

Today was the sixteenth day of my Data Analysis training. The session focused on **Microsoft Power BI**, a powerful business intelligence and data visualization tool used to analyze data and create interactive dashboards and reports.

The session began with an introduction to **Power BI** and its applications in data analysis and business intelligence. I learned about the different components of Power BI, including **Power BI Desktop**, **Power BI Service**, and **Power BI Mobile**, and understood how they work together to create and share reports.

Next, I learned how to **import data** from various sources, such as Excel files and CSV files, into Power BI. The trainer explained how to preview datasets, load data, and prepare it for analysis.

The trainer then introduced the **Power Query Editor**, where I learned basic data transformation techniques such as removing duplicate records, filtering rows, changing data types, renaming columns, and handling missing values. These preprocessing steps are essential for ensuring accurate data analysis.

After that, I explored different **visualization tools** available in Power BI. I practiced creating **bar charts, line charts, pie charts, tables, cards, and slicers** to present data in a meaningful and interactive way. I also learned how to customize charts by modifying titles, colors, labels, legends, and formatting options.

The session also covered the concept of **dashboards and reports**. I understood how to combine multiple visualizations into a single interactive report, allowing users to explore data using filters and slicers. This helps in identifying trends, patterns, and insights more effectively.

During the practical session, I created a basic Power BI report by importing a dataset, performing simple data transformations, and designing visualizations to represent key information. This hands-on activity improved my understanding of data visualization and report creation.

Overall, today's session provided a strong foundation in Power BI. I gained practical experience in importing, transforming, analyzing, and visualizing data, as well as creating interactive reports and dashboards. These skills are essential for business intelligence, data analysis, and making data-driven decisions in real-world projects.
